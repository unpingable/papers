## The primitive

A civic / institutional speech regime in which **universal inclusion (or
performance, or safety, or compliance) is admissible at coarse resolution,
while finer distinctions needed to identify exclusion (or shortfall, or
risk, or violation) are made socially or procedurally inadmissible.**

Signature: **resolution-selective suppression**. The coarse version of a
topic is welcomed, the fine version is disciplined, and no merits rebuttal
is offered. The act of refining is what gets disciplined, not the refined
claim's content.

## Three compact forms

> **Formal:** The regime forbids the resolution at which its claim becomes
> falsifiable.

> **Operational (audit-handle):** The coarse map is socially or procedurally
> protected from refinement.

> **Discriminator:** When R+1 is raised, the response is not to engage the
> measurement; the response is to discipline the act of measuring.

## Discriminator stack (the three-level taxonomy)

NI sits inside a larger genus and against a passive sibling. The full
distinction stack:

| Level                       | Mechanism                                                                                              |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **P25** (passive)           | The map is too coarse. Target mistaken for proxy. No bad faith required.                               |
| **Observability laundering** (active, genus) | The map is **kept** coarse by design. Active narrowing of the observable subspace disguised as ordinary measurement, compliance, or governance. |
| **Negative inclusivity** (active + governed, species) | The coarse map is **socially or procedurally protected** from the refinement that would falsify it. Disciplining refinement is the species marker. |

Named species under observability laundering:

- **Negative inclusivity** — speech / governance species. Coarse map socially
  or procedurally protected from refinement. Discipline of refinement-
  requesters is the load-bearing mechanism.
- **VW-pattern** — technical-deception species. System engineered to satisfy

  engineered sensor clamping, not social protection.
- **MAX-pattern** — contractual / certification species. Candidate NI
  subspecies. Coarse map (common type rating, training-impact minimization)

The species split matters because future cases on the active-observability
axis should not get crammed under NI just because they fit the genus.
VW is the worked exemplar of the technical-deception species; MAX is the
worked exemplar of the contractual/certification species.

## Goodhart / Campbell positioning

NI is prior-art-extending on Goodhart's Law and Campbell's Law, not
prior-art-replacing.

> Goodhart and Campbell explain **distortion** under metric pressure: any
> metric that becomes a target ceases to be a good metric.
> NI and the broader observability-laundering genus explain **durability**:
> the governance pattern that keeps the distorted metric admissible when
> refinement pressure appears.

Metric distortion is well-known; the question NI uniquely names is *why
gamed metrics stay institutionally valid rather than self-correcting under
refinement*. The answer: refinement is procedurally inadmissible, and
refinement-requesters bear social cost. Goodhart explains the failure
mode; NI explains why the failure mode is stable.

This positioning is load-bearing for the Substack venue and for academic
legibility — NI is not a repackaging of Goodhart; it is the missing
sociology of why Goodhart-distortion persists.

## Cross-domain packet (gate-passing instances)

### 1. Wells Fargo cross-sell metric — lead specimen (corporate KPI)

- **Coarse claim:** "Customers have deeper relationships with us."
- **Admissible resolution:** products per household / cross-sell count.
- **Forbidden finer resolution:** authorized vs unauthorized, wanted vs
  unwanted, used vs unused, customer-need-driven vs quota-driven.
- **Active-protection evidence:** CFPB (2016) — employees opened
  unauthorized accounts to hit sales targets; SEC — public cross-sell
  disclosures materially misleading because the metric included
  accounts/products not needed, wanted, or used; reporting (NPR and
  others) — employees who called the ethics line to whistleblow were
  fired or career-damaged.
- **Gate sentence:** *The customer-relationship map stayed institutionally
  valid only while account existence was allowed to stand in for customer
  assent.*

### 2. NYPD CompStat / Schoolcraft — civic metric specimen

- **Coarse claim:** "Crime is down."
- **Admissible resolution:** reported index crime counts.
- **Forbidden finer resolution:** complaint-taking friction, downgrading of
  classifications, discouragement of reports, precinct-level pressure.
- **Active-protection evidence:** NYPD internal investigation validated
  Adrian Schoolcraft's whistleblower claim that his precinct systematically
  underreported crime — *and* Schoolcraft was suspended and confined in a
  psychiatric ward after recording precinct practices. Involuntary
  psychiatric commitment of the refinement-requester is the strongest
  single piece of active-protection evidence in the packet.
- **Gate sentence:** *The crime-rate map remained politically usable while
  the classification machinery that produced it was made costly to inspect
  — costly up to and including involuntary commitment.*

### 3. BP / Transocean safety metrics — safety-reporting specimen

- **Coarse claim:** "We are safe."
- **Admissible resolution:** personal injury rates, lost-time incidents,
  slips/trips/falls.
- **Forbidden / devalued finer resolution:** process safety, blowout risk,
  barrier health, well-control risk, catastrophic-incident precursors.
- **Active-protection evidence:** CSB explicitly found BP and Transocean
  had VIPs on Deepwater Horizon partly to commend zero lost-time incidents
  on the day of the disaster. *And* — load-bearing for the NI-vs-P25
  distinction — CSB ties the 2010 blowout back to Texas City 2005, where
  workers had just returned from a celebratory lunch for an excellent
  personal-injury record before an explosion killed 15 and injured 180.
  The refinement (process safety as distinct axis) was already named in
  CSB findings from 2005; the institutional non-adoption from 2005 to 2010
  is what crosses from passive P25 ("we didn't think to measure that") to
  active protection ("the celebrated metric stayed celebrated even after
  the refinement was published in our own regulator's reports").
- **Gate sentence:** *The safety claim remained admissible at the injury-
  count resolution while the process-safety resolution stayed devalued
  across two near-identical celebratory-lunch fatalities five years apart.*

### Second-tier instances (corroborating, not packet-load-bearing)

- **Atlanta Public Schools cheating scandal.** 178 educators implicated
  across 44 of 56 schools investigated; Georgia investigation found a
  "culture of fear, intimidation, and retaliation"; employees who refused
  to cheat were punished. Test-score map protected; learning vs score-
  production refinement procedurally inadmissible. Campbell's Law in a
  superintendent lanyard.
- **VA wait-time scandal.** OIG investigated 93 sites and reported wait-
  time manipulation was prevalent; schedulers falsified records and
  obstructed investigators. Strong observability-laundering specimen; NI
  if the retaliation-against-refiners thread is emphasized.

### Platform worked instance (explicitly outside cross-domain proof)

- **Bluesky / labelers / composable moderation.** Coarse: "moderation is
  decentralized/composable." Fine: labels are testimony, enforcement
  remains elsewhere (defaults, hosting, discoverability, social pressure).
  Refinement-requesters get treated as hostile to community safety. Fits
  the NI shape. Held outside the cross-domain proof because it shares
  substrate with AI labeling and Aegis-style safety infra — one platform-
  governance domain three ways. Cited as **contemporary worked instance**,
  not as a third independent domain.

## Sibling species and borderline cases

### VW emissions — technical-deception species (not NI)

- **Coarse claim:** "Vehicles meet emissions standards."
- **Admissible resolution:** lab / test-cycle emissions.
- **Clamped finer resolution:** real-world driving emissions.
- **Why not NI:** EPA says VW software detected emissions testing and
  enabled full controls only during the test, with NOx up to 40× the
  standard during normal driving. Mechanism is engineered sensor clamping,
  not social protection of test-cycle compliance as the relevant resolution.
  This is the technical-deception species under the observability-laundering
  genus.

### Boeing 737 MAX — contractual / certification species (candidate NI subspecies)

- **Coarse claim:** "Still basically a 737; no major new pilot-training
  burden."
- **Admissible resolution:** common type rating / differences training.
- **Suppressed finer resolution:** MCAS behavior, failure modes, simulator
  training need.
- **Active-protection evidence:** December 2011 Southwest Airlines contract
  with Boeing — at least **$1 million per delivered MAX** payable to
  Southwest if simulator training was required, against 200 firm orders
  plus 191 options. No-simulator-training was a stated design objective
  of the program. Source: House Transportation & Infrastructure Committee
  final report, *The Boeing 737 MAX Aircraft: Costs, Consequences, and
  Lessons from its Design, Development, and Certification* (Sept. 2020).
  DOT OIG separately found that minimizing training and certification
  impacts was a Boeing program goal documented in internal materials.
- **Why NI subspecies (not just observability laundering):** the no-
  simulator-training coarse map was protected by a **contractual** penalty
  for refinement — financial cost imposed on the act of requiring the
  refined resolution. This is structurally the same mechanism as NI's
  "social cost for asking" — same protection-of-coarse-map shape, different
  enforcement surface (contract / certification rather than social
  discourse). Argues for a contractual/certification species sitting
  alongside the speech/governance species under the genus.
- **Gate-altitude assessment:** worth promoting from "adjacent" to NI
  subspecies once the House T&I citations are folded into the working
  note. Carries the framework cleanly into hard-engineering territory and
  blunts any objection that NI is only a discourse phenomenon.

### Civic / speech-regime probes (held out)

- **"Colorblind" anti-CRT / divisive-concepts laws.** Very NI-shaped:
  coarse claim is formal equality, forbidden refinement is institutional
  causality / disparate impact. Politically radioactive; held out of the
  packet to keep the primitive's introduction from being eaten by the
  discourse.
- **Parental Rights / "Don't Say Gay"-style instruction bans.** Same shape,
  same heat, same hold-out reasoning.

These are flagged in the working note as **probes for later civic-writing
work, not pillars for the primitive's introduction.**

## Rule-outs (the discriminator earns its keep)

The primitive must rule out tempting false positives where surface looks
coarse-vs-fine but the merits-rebuttal-is-offered test reveals honest
disagreement or legitimate aggregation. Three earned rule-outs:

- **Rule-out A — honest aggregation with drilldown.** Exec dashboard says
  "service healthy" but p99 latency, regional error rate, and customer-
  impact drilldowns exist, are reviewed, and can override the summary.
  **Not NI:** the coarse map is compressed for venue, but refinement is
  admissible and acted upon.
- **Rule-out B — privacy-preserving demographic aggregation.** Organization
  refuses public subgroup breakdown because sample size is small and
  reidentification risk is real, but allows trusted third-party audit or
  confidential internal review. **Not NI:** merits rebuttal offered; an
  alternate refinement path exists.
- **Rule-out C — live scientific dispute.** A field rejects a finer
  distinction because evidence is not yet strong enough, publishes the
  criteria, engages counterevidence, and later revises. **Not NI:** the
  refinement is engaged on its merits, not made procedurally inadmissible.

Each rule-out becomes NI only when the missing piece appears: refinement
not rebutted but made costly to raise.

## Litmus questions

For a candidate instance:

1. Is the coarse claim welcomed in the venue?
2. Is there a refinement R+1 at which the coarse claim becomes test-
   falsifiable?
3. When R+1 is raised: is the response a merits rebuttal, or is the *act
   of raising R+1* what gets disciplined? (Discipline can be social,
   procedural, contractual, or — at the Schoolcraft extreme —
   psychiatric.)
4. Does a privileged subset retain access to R+1 internally while the
   public register stays at R? (Optional sovereign-asymmetry marker,
   parallel to CSL.)

If 1, 2, and 3 hold with 3 returning discipline rather than rebuttal, the
instance fits. Sovereign-asymmetry (4) is a power-marker, not a requirement.

## What this is NOT

- Not a theorem. The mechanism description is descriptive, not proved.
- Not authorization to build an observability-laundering Lean module.
  Primitive-naming gate passed; formal-promotion gate is separate.
- Not a synonym for P25. P25's narrowness is passive; NI's narrowness is
  active and governed.
- Not a synonym for Goodhart. Goodhart explains distortion; NI explains
  durability of distortion under refinement pressure.
- Not a license to relabel all consensus-seeking civic speech as NI. The
  load-bearing discriminator is **no merits rebuttal at the refined
  resolution** — if the refined claim is engaged on its merits and refuted
  or held pending evidence, that is honest disagreement (or live science),
  not NI.
- Not exclusively political-domain. The mechanism is general
  (resolution-clamping with social protection); the cross-domain packet
  spans corporate KPI, civic metric, safety reporting.
- Not promoted to paper-slot or Lean module. Working note is canonical.
