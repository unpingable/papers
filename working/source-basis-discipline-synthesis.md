# Source-basis discipline — synthesis candidate

> **Status:** synthesis candidate / post-three-specimen review / not ratified calculus doctrine.
>
> **Scope:** records convergence and unresolved divergence after three formal specimens; opens no new axis.

This note preserves the signal from a four-node review pass
following the survival of three Lean specimens at
`~/git/lean/LeanProofs/Admissibility/`: AmendmentFragment,
ContractionHinge, RetroactiveLegitimation. The purpose is to
capture what converged without promoting any of it to "the maximal
calculus." The convergence is real enough to file; the file is not
closure.

## Core claim

> **Admissibility is source-basis discipline: a judgment must be
> discharged from the basis it is entitled to use, and cannot be
> discharged by creating, duplicating, retrojecting, or substituting
> that basis inside the move being justified.**

## Per-specimen instantiation

- **AmendmentFragment** — wrong basis: post-state or self-created
  policy substituted for source-state authority.
- **ContractionHinge** — wrong basis: one warrant occurrence
  substituted for two required occurrences.
- **RetroactiveLegitimation** — wrong basis: post-state witness
  substituted for pre-state authorization.

## Three-altitude distinction (do not collapse)

The four candidates from the review pass live at different levels;
they are recorded with their sources because the convergence's
meaning depends on the multi-node provenance.

1. **Anatomy candidate** — `Context / State / Warrant / Operation
   → Judgment`.
   - Source: Claude Code.
   - Status: unratified anatomy candidate.
2. **Mechanism candidate** — refusal to discharge by collapsing
   an index.
   - Source: Claude-web.
   - Status: unratified mechanism candidate.
3. **Proof-shape candidate** — invariant-based impossibility /
   source-precondition invariant safety.
   - Source: DeepSeek.
   - Status: independent non-Claude synthesis signal.
4. **Public-facing / governance phrase** — acyclic justification /
   conservation of authority.
   - Source: Gemini.
   - Status: useful phrase, but "authority" is too broad for the
     formal line because ContractionHinge is about warrant
     occurrence, not policy authority. Will likely smear
     fragment-specific distinctions if promoted to formal
     vocabulary.

The fused "source-basis discipline" keeper phrase was assembled by
ChatGPT from these four; it is not itself a fifth altitude but a
working name for what the four roughly converge on.

## Caveat — the convergence is not cleanly one-axis

> The convergence is real but not cleanly one-axis. At a lower
> altitude the specimens appear to split into:
>
> - a **temporal/source pair**: AmendmentFragment and
>   RetroactiveLegitimation;
> - a **multiplicity/resource case**: ContractionHinge.
>
> Whether "source-basis discipline" is a genuine unification or
> the appearance of one created by averaging over two
> refusal-shapes remains unresolved.

## Common-mode warning

> The two-axis caveat was identified by Claude Code after reading
> Claude-web, ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Gemini, and the artifacts. Because
> this is synthesis-altitude judgment from a Claude node, it should
> be treated as a filed caveat, not independent confirmation.
> Ratification requires a later non-Claude adversarial pass.

## Keeper line

> **A move is inadmissible when it can only justify itself by
> spending a basis it has not yet earned, duplicating a basis it
> was given once, or retroactively importing a basis created by
> its own result.**

## What this file does NOT claim

- The maximal calculus exists.
- The four candidates have been reduced to one.
- The keeper line is doctrine.
- Source-basis discipline has a Lean shape.
- "Conservation of authority" is the formal vocabulary.
- The two-axis caveat is independently verified.
