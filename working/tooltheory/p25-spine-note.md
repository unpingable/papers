# P25 forcing theorem — the constellation as substitution-refusal architecture

**Status:** spine note (current-load-bearing framing; use, don't re-derive).
*Migrated from Claude memory on 2026-06-22 (`project-p25-spine-note`).*

The cross-repo synthesis that snapped into focus 2026-04-27 (ag-claude review of P25
candidates, ChatGPT's synthesis):

**P25's forcing theorem.** Under observability asymmetry — when no real-time sensor on
the controlled variable V exists — substitution is *structurally forced*. The controller
must regulate a proxy V′; the rhetoric stays on V. This is necessity-framed, not
Goodhart-framed: substitution happens whether or not anyone is gaming.

**The constellation as architectural reply.** The six non-collapsible boundaries (NQ /
Cadence / Continuity / Custody / Standing / Governor, with Δm as predicted seventh) are
not a denial of the forcing. They are the architectural refusal: hold multiple distinct
V-sensors so no single observability asymmetry forces system-wide substitution.
Substitution must be **explicit, bounded, plural, contestable, and decaying.** That is
the difference between governance and theater.

**Per-instantiation refusal map (application-layer — illustrates the spine, isn't part
of it):** the doctrine doesn't depend on these specific habitats; they happen to be
where the forcing/refusal pattern was first made operational.

- **NQ** refuses "looks healthy" as a substitute for state. Carries ambiguity rather than
  laundering it.
- **Standing** refuses credentials as a substitute for realized controller identity.
- **Continuity** refuses "latest memory" as a substitute for reliable memory unless TTL,
  basis, and revalidation exist.
- **Custody** refuses "reviewed" as a substitute for valid review.
- **Interferometry** refuses consensus as a substitute for independent sight.
- **Governor** refuses intent as a substitute for admissible authority.

**The escalating-negative-results sequence (the series shape):**

- **P22:** regulation can outrun what it regulates (no universal plant clock).
- **P23:** the controller may not be self-identical (controller-layer masking).
- **P24:** the witness population may be structurally wrong even with feedback, dissent,
  and updating (aggregation-layer masking).
- **P25 candidate:** the target may be unsensed; substitution is forced (epistemic border
  control).
- **P26 candidate:** the system's own temporal seam may fail — premature admissibility,
  belated consequence (m(t)/c(t) duality).

Each paper names one structural assumption that "the system" makes about itself and shows
it is not free. The constellation is what computable refusal of those assumptions looks
like.

**P27 as the constructive closer (queue-jumped 2026-04-28).** P22–P26 is escalating
*negative* results. P27 is the *constructive* answer to the part of the problem that
admits one: given that you can't recover what isn't sensed (P25), at minimum govern
transitions so the controller does not silently destroy what *was* sensed. Spine line:
**P25 says you can't recover what isn't sensed; P27 says govern transitions so the
controller does not silently destroy what was sensed.** Different knife (transition
admissibility / obligation soundness across reconciliation), same drawer
(observability-vs-truth). P27 imports projection from P25 and horizons from P26 without
absorbing either — they remain distinct papers with their own knives.

**The recursion risk to track.** NQ punishes scalar collapse in observed systems, but its
detector portfolio, platform coverage, and "what we bother to instrument" choices are
themselves a witness sample that can be alignment-correlated. P24 bites the observer too.
The constellation must apply to itself.

**The portable-doctrine claim.** Agent_gov was the first habitat with enough pressure to
make these invariants load-bearing — it forced the theory to become operational. The
theory now walks on its own: P23/P24 are not AG-specific. The papers are the theory; AG,
NQ, Standing, Continuity, Night Shift are the operational habitats where the theory was
first compelled into formality.

**How to apply.** When asked about cross-repo connections, paper-to-instantiation maps,
or where new structural claims fit in the series, use this spine — don't re-derive. The
forcing/refusal frame also tells you when a candidate paper is in scope: it either (a)
sharpens the forcing (a new mode of substitution-pressure), (b) extends the refusal
architecture (a new boundary or witness-discipline), or (c) names a reflexive failure of
the architecture itself.
