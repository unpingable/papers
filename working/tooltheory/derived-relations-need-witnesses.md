## The law

> Some operational claims are **relations over witnessed objects**, not properties
> recoverable from those objects individually.

> **Refusal:** a derived relation may NOT be promoted from the conjunction of its
> endpoints' receipts. The relation is its own claim and needs its own admitted
> witness. Absent that witness, the honest output is `cannot_testify` — not the
> conjunction of the endpoint verdicts.

This is the no-free-lift move with the "lift" running *endpoints → relation*. The
endpoints each carry valid receipts; the relation between them does not inherit one.

## The three instances (the recurrence that earns the name)

1. **clock-witness** *(AG-custody sibling)* — `gap(A, B)` is not arithmetic over two
   timestamps; it is a **licensed comparison between compatible clock witnesses**.
   Two valid timestamps do not license their difference.
2. **projection-receipt** *(AG-custody sibling)* — `read(surface)` is not stored
   truth; it is a **witnessed projection across a boundary**. A value present on a
   surface does not carry the receipt that admitted it across.
3. **differential-witness** *(this corpus; the third member)* — `verdict(A) ∧
   verdict(B)` is not an explanation of **A-vs-B**. The differential treatment is its
   own claim; absent a `RelationReceipt` (comparator / discriminator / ordering /
   policy-version), the honest output about the *difference* is `cannot_testify`.

Instances 1–2 were filed AG-side this week; this note is the tooltheory home for the
**shared law**, cross-referencing them — it is NOT a second copy of their custody.

## Non-claims (the fences — these matter more than the law)

- **The differential instance is PRIOR-ART-DEFLATED, not a new axis.** Its specific
  content is occupied by Kroll procedural regularity + Dwork individual fairness +
  non-interference (checked + codex-confirmed 2026-06-19; see the memory tombstone
  `project-distributional-admissibility-tombstone`). Recognizing it as instance #3 of
  this family does **not** re-inflate it into "distributional admissibility." It stays
  deflated; the family is the receipt-discipline framing, not a new property.
- **The contribution is the receipt discipline, NOT the structural observation.**
  "A relation is not recoverable from its endpoints" has a relational-verification /
  2-safety / hyperproperty shadow (Clarkson–Schneider, self-composition) — that
  structure is occupied. What is ours is the *artifact-lifecycle* framing: relations
  get first-class admitted witnesses with provenance and expiry, like any other
  receipt. Do not sell the quantifier structure as the novelty.
- **Not claimdocs semantics.** claimdocs governs doc-graph edges; a differential
  auditor operates on runtime decision records. Folding decision-pair logic into
  claimdocs because the grammar smells similar (`cannot_testify` / UNKNOWN-poisons-PASS)
  is substrate contamination — the same fence claimdocs itself enforces. Shared
  grammar, different substrate.
- **Not NQ core, not a kernel, not a build mandate.** No live forcing consumer points
  at a decision log as of 2026-06-19.

## If/when built (parked until a forcing consumer)

- **`RelationReceipt`** is the artifact: subject = a relation between decision records;
  admitted_basis = comparator / policy-version / ordering / randomness / quota; it
  *says* "this differential treatment was licensed by this admitted relation" and
  **`does_not_say`** globally-fair / substantively-just / optimal / legally-
  nondiscriminatory.
- **Preferred specimen = load-shedding** (a machine picking victims under scarcity),
  NOT shadowban (which drags in intent / opacity / moderation politics — more perfume,
  less skeleton). The rate-limit/quota variant rides LA (the capacity office already
  has consume/expiry receipts); the *relational wrapper* is the only new part.
- **Name fence:** `differential-witness` / `RelationReceipt` / `why-different`. Avoid
  `diff` — AG already ships `claim_diff` (diffing the epistemic ledger over time:
  confidence drift, silent retraction), a different axis; reusing "diff" confuses them.
  And not `NoSilentDiscriminator` except as a deflated-specimen alias.
