## Core sentence

> **Negative inclusivity** is a civic / institutional speech regime in which universal inclusion is admissible at coarse resolution while finer distinctions needed to identify exclusion, domination, or structural harm are made socially or procedurally inadmissible.

It differs from a hermeneutical gap: *the vocabulary exists; its use is enclosed.* The institution does not lack the distinction — **it prices the distinction out of feasible speech.**

## Cybernetic gloss (keeper)

> **Negative inclusivity performs openness by displaying an input channel, then preserves stability by disabling the sensors capable of error correction.**

Self-anchoring. Same shape as the consequence-partition keeper (*"composable classification with centralized consequence is theater"*) but at civic-institutional altitude. Both name the gap between formal openness and actual correction-bearing capacity.

## Diagnostic predicate (5-clause)

```text
NegativeInclusivity exists when:
  Coarse(topic)                       is admissible
  AND Fine(topic, harm_distinction)   is suppressed
  AND Fine                            is checkable / consequence-bearing
  AND suppression                     occurs without merits rebuttal
  AND suppression                     is justified as civility, unity, scope, or procedure
```

Clause 3 ("Fine is checkable / consequence-bearing") is the load-bearing distinguisher from civility-as-bandwidth: what gets clipped is specifically the *corrective* signal, not the *noisy* one.

## State machine

| State | Role |
|---|---|
| **Coarse Civility** | Low-resolution frame used for meeting hygiene. *Baseline, not pathology.* |
| **Bureaucratic Deferral** | Fine signal acknowledged, routed elsewhere. *Honest-or-not depending on whether the route discharges.* |
| **Negative Inclusivity** | Coarse positive frame admitted; fine corrective frame suppressed. **The kernel pathology.** |
| **Hermeneutical Enclosure** | Sensors self-silence because the cost wall is anticipated. **Terminal equilibrium.** |

**Transition trigger:**

> **Refinement becomes obligation-bearing.**

The institution does not merely lack bandwidth; it recognizes that admitting the fine signal would force correction, record, routing, exception, vote change, delay, or explicit refusal. That recognition flips Bureaucratic Deferral into Negative Inclusivity. NI plus `confidence_decay` converges to Hermeneutical Enclosure: **NI is the mechanism, HE is the equilibrium.** The institution wins by making the speakers stop.

**Variables:** `resolution`, `obligation`, `admissibility`, `cost`, `correction`, `confidence_decay`. Not extended here — *that's enough model.*

## Relation to redress-as-containment

Redress channels can perform the formal shape of resolution without producing its function because the channel controls the resolution at which grievance may appear. The redress channel is the projection gate operationalized: it accepts the *coarse* form of the grievance, refuses the *fine* form, and the refusal is justified procedurally.

## What's new beyond ConsequencePartition (sibling, not instance)

Three pieces that CP does not carry:

1. **Temporal dynamics.** State machine + `confidence_decay`. CP is point-in-time. NI has a terminal state (HE) reached through repeated operation; that convergence is the actual deepest pathology, and CP has no analog.
2. **Speaker-side cost.** CP's `Surface S A` has feasibility as an *action* property; NI's cost falls on the *speaker* whose fine signal gets penalized. Different cost-bearer; different actor on whom the wall rises.
3. **Substrate-justification clause.** Predicate clause 5 explicitly requires suppression be *framed as civility / unity / scope / procedure*. CP has no substrate-justification requirement.

These differences make NI a *probable sibling*, not a re-derivation. Resolution requires the kernel-overlap audit (deferred per anti-goals).
