# Anti-Collapse Runbook

> *Sometimes the glow is uranium. Sometimes it is a Mountain Dew bottle under a blacklight. Operator has to check before building a shrine.*

**Status:** working note / operator runbook. Containment protocol for the operator's generative loop. Not a primitive. Not a process to be followed verbatim. **Reading posture and triage discipline** for situations where a conversation throws off shiny field-shaped fragments and the operator has to avoid founding three accidental republics before lunch.

**Originated:** 2026-05-10 multi-model conversation. The *triangulation-as-anti-collapse* discipline at the runbook's center was crystallized by ChatGPT from operational moves implicit across the session's substitution-laundering work; ChatGPT then reframed the discipline as a runbook (procedure + triage + failure modes + reflexivity check); claude-2 added the *mirror poisoning* failure mode and the *audit the auditor once, do not recurse forever* reflexivity discipline.

**Sibling operator doctrine** (parent class: *Operator Doctrine*; siblings classified by *failure mode prevented*, not by audience): [claim-shape-brain-stack](claim-shape-brain-stack.md) protects charge/remedy integrity; [narrow-after-contact](narrow-after-contact.md) protects against phase-misaligned narrowing; [falsification-guardrails](../docs/method/falsification.md) protects framework scope from universal-solvent drift; note-density discipline (in user memory) protects artifacts from overbuilt staging. **This runbook protects against axis-impersonation laundering during generative idea production.** Same audience, distinct vetoes.

## What this runbook is and is not

This is a **containment protocol** for the operator's generative loop. It is not a process to be followed step-by-step. It is the discipline for *when conversations start throwing off shiny field-shaped fragments* — and the operator has to triage what to file, what to discard, what's a useful specimen, what's a doctrine candidate, and what's a Mountain Dew bottle.

The Large Idea Collider needs containment because its whole job is smashing concepts together until something glows. The runbook exists so the operator checks the glow source before building a shrine.

This is **not**:

- A theory of synthesis or argumentation
- A philosophical posture against unification
- A taxonomy for filing every idea that surfaces
- A procedure that can be applied without judgment

It is operator-side workflow doctrine — sibling to claim-shape-brain-stack and narrow-after-contact — for the specific failure mode of *axis-impersonation laundering during generative idea production*.

## Section 1: Anti-collapse pass

The central technique. **Triangulation here means preserving independent axes of judgment so one kind of claim cannot impersonate another** — explicitly *not* the standard *converge multiple perspectives on truth* sense.

> *Standard triangulation: multiple lines converge on a truth.*
>
> *Triangulation as anti-collapse: multiple axes are held apart so one cannot impersonate another.*

**Use when** a discussion produces a compelling synthesis, new field-name, taxonomy, doctrine sibling, or *this explains everything* candidate.

**Procedure:**

1. **Separate the axes.**
   - What kind of claim is this?
   - epistemic / material / political
   - descriptive / prescriptive / operational
   - evidence / authority / obligation
   - access / consent / reliance
2. **Locate the substitution.**
   - What is being treated as equivalent?
   - What weaker claim is borrowing authority from a stronger one?
   - What technical artifact is being mistaken for social permission?
3. **Refuse premature synthesis.**
   - Do not reconcile tension until the laundering move is visible.
   - Do not promote contradiction into *higher-order framing* unless it preserves consequence.
4. **Assign consequence only after the axes stop impersonating each other.** Premature synthesis is laundering with extra steps.

**Operator rule:**

> *Do not synthesize until the substitutions are visible.*

### Canonical axis pairs (do not let these collapse)

These are the substitution patterns the discipline is designed to catch. Each `≠` is a load-bearing inequality; collapse in either direction is the failure mode.

- **authenticity ≠ availability ≠ reliance** — *signed* doesn't entail *available*; *available* doesn't entail *relied upon*. (See [authority-observable-not-constructible](authority-observable-not-constructible.md) and its sibling-doctrine candidate on reliance.)
- **access ≠ consent ≠ obligation** — being able to reach a resource doesn't entail consent to its use; consent doesn't entail enforceable obligation.
- **diagnosis ≠ accountability ≠ repair** — naming what went wrong doesn't entail assigning who owes consequence; assigning consequence doesn't entail fixing the harm.
- **visibility ≠ standing ≠ authority** — being seen doesn't entail standing to bind; standing doesn't entail authority to act.
- **explanation ≠ justification ≠ absolution** — describing why doesn't entail justifying; justifying doesn't entail discharging consequence. *This last one has teeth.*

The list is not exhaustive. New axis pairs surface as substitutions are caught; the catalog is a *diagnostic register*, not a fixed taxonomy.

### The *what did you do and to whom* connection

The simplest practical form of the anti-collapse pass is the question: **what did you do and to whom?** That question drops below the laundered frame. It refuses the *higher understanding* move. No therapeutic vapor, no institutional mist, no *we all participated in a complex dynamic.* Just: action, object, consequence, standing.

When a frame is doing laundering work, the *what did you do and to whom* question is the operational anti-collapse pass — it forces the axes (action / object / consequence / standing) apart and prevents them from being absorbed into a synthesizing vocabulary.

## Section 2: Filing-level triage

After the anti-collapse pass, the surviving fragment needs to be filed at the right level. **Don't promote glowing fragments too early.** Most idea-management failure modes are filing-level errors, not content errors.

Filing levels, in order of escalating commitment:

1. **Conversation-only** — the keeper survives in this thread but doesn't land on disk. Minimum minimum.
2. **Bad-idea specimen** — useful as an example of what *not* to do. Filed in a bad-ideas register or as a sibling pointer in a relevant working note.
3. **Operator discipline** — workflow / posture / reading-stance material. Lands in working/ alongside claim-shape-brain-stack, narrow-after-contact, this runbook.
4. **Sibling pointer** — extends an existing doctrine without minting a new one. Lands as a new section in the existing parent file (e.g., the *reliance observable, not constructible* sibling in `authority-observable-not-constructible.md`).
5. **Gap-spec / candidate** — earns a candidate-shapes inventory entry; not yet a working note of its own. Re-prune trigger required.
6. **Doctrine** — earns its own working note in working/. Default-project-note density unless bridging multiple already-mature converging registers.
7. **Paper / book primitive** — earns a numbered paper or book chapter entry. Forcing case required.

**Triage rules:**

- **Name the load-bearing sentence.** If no sentence survives compression, do not file.
- **If only a joke survives, file as joke/specimen, not doctrine.** *Hegelsop* was funny in the lab, wrong on disk. *Large Idea Collider* survives as runbook framing because it's also accurate. Different threshold.
- **Default to the lowest filing level that preserves the load-bearing claim.** Promotion is reversible only with effort.

## Section 3: Failure modes

The named failure modes the runbook is designed to catch. Each is a real pattern, not theoretical.

- **Field minting.** A useful distinction gets promoted into a discipline. *Dependency calculus*, *quorum cybernetics as field*, *anti-collapse cybernetics*. The fix is naming the distinction without minting the field.
- **Cube poisoning.** A triad becomes a 3×3 because *symmetry felt hungry*. Naming this prevents the temporal-anatomy reframe and the modal-axis triad from being multiplied into a cube *because the cells looked empty*.
- **Terminology laundering.** Naming an aid like a guarantee, a convenience like infrastructure, or access like consent. Same shape as the Consequence-Structure Substitutions taxonomy in `primitives/attack-surface-laundering.md`; the failure mode here is the *naming step* that enables the substitution.
- **Synthesis slop.** Contradiction gets promoted instead of answered. *We've moved to a higher understanding* where somehow the original accountability question dissolved into the new frame. The Hegel-shaped failure mode.
- **Doctrine sprawl.** A sibling pointer becomes a standalone doctrine before it has independent load. The fix is the filing-level triage above.
- **Mirror poisoning.** *(Defensive-recursion failure mode added by claude-2.)* The diagnostic language for detecting laundering is reflected back into the dispute as a laundering move. The critique becomes an instrument of the thing it names. *You're doing semantic laundering* used as semantic laundering. *You're collapsing axes* while collapsing axes. DARVO-adjacent but broader: it applies whenever an anti-laundering vocabulary becomes available as a counter-laundering instrument.

## Section 4: Reflexivity check

The runbook is not immune to the failures it detects. Before filing or escalating a claim, check whether the procedure itself is being used as a laundering surface:

- Is *locate the substitution* being used to substitute away the original consequence?
- Is *anti-synthesis* being used to refuse a legitimate resolution?
- Is *triangulation* being used to multiply axes until no verdict is possible?
- Is the critique of a frame being used to seize control of the frame?

**Operator rule:**

> *Audit the auditor once. Do not recurse forever.*

If the reflexivity check does not produce a clearer substitution, **lower the filing level rather than minting a meta-doctrine.** Otherwise the Large Idea Collider becomes a Large Meta-Idea Collider, which is just graduate school with better logging.

## Why this is distinct from claim-shape-brain-stack and narrow-after-contact

All three are operator-side doctrine. They are not the same doctrine.

- **Claim-shape brain** asks: *did this fact still prosecute the charge, or did it change the remedy surface?* Governs argument integrity under remedy contamination.
- **Narrow-after-contact** asks: *is narrowing appropriate for the phase I'm in?* Governs production-side calibration of when to trim.
- **This runbook** asks: *what just glowed, what kind of glow is it, and where should it be filed?* Governs reading posture + triage during generative idea production.

The runbook runs *most upstream* of the three: triangulation surfaces what's actually being claimed, triage decides what to do with it, then claim-shape-brain and narrow-after-contact apply to whatever survives filing.

Per the *same audience does not imply same doctrine* meta-principle from `narrow-after-contact.md`: distinct sibling notes, classified by failure mode prevented.

## Anti-scope clauses

This runbook **does not**:

- Argue against synthesis as a general intellectual operation. *Some* synthesis is correct and load-bearing; the discipline is against synthesis that operates *before* substitutions have been made visible.
- Promote anti-synthesis as a philosophical posture. That becomes its own vibe (*Hegelsop* — funny in the lab, wrong on disk). The discipline is *operational anti-collapse against semantic substitution*, not general suspicion of unification.
- Provide a procedure that can be followed without judgment. The triage rules require operator judgment at every step; the runbook reduces variance, not eliminates it.
- Promote to a primitive. This is method/posture, sibling to claim-shape-brain-stack, narrow-after-contact, falsification-guardrails. Operator-side workflow doctrine, not corpus primitive.
- Generalize to all reading. The discipline is most useful when *frames are doing accountability-deflection work* OR *generative conversations are throwing off field-shaped fragments*. Overkill for ordinary technical disagreement.
