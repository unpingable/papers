This pilot's job is to *record that the refusal still holds* under a sharper question (NI ↔ AAG sacrificial-escape rather than relay-shape ↔ composition-axis) and to specify *what the refusal protects*. The refusal protects:

1. **NI's mechanism — active suppression of refinement.** NI catches systems where the coarse map is socially / procedurally / contractually / psychiatrically protected from R+1 falsification. The mechanism is *external imposition* on the refinement-requester (discipline of refinement is the species marker). The system *stays at coarse map*; nothing exits anything.
2. **AAG sacrificial-escape's mechanism — capability burned to exit basin.** AAG sacrificial-escape catches the recovery-audit case where a system has *already recognized* it is in an inadmissible attractor and is exiting via structural cost. The mechanism is *internal choice* by the system to burn capability (limb, tissue, structure, standing) to escape. The system *exits*; nothing is being suppressed.

These are not the same shape. They are:
- **NI** = pre-event regime in which the trap stays unobserved because R+1 is procedurally inadmissible
- **AAG sacrificial-escape** = post-event recovery audit in which exit-via-capability-burn is refused as restoration

The relay's "sacrifice-floor" framing collapses the two by treating both as *burden imposed at a threshold*. That collapse is exactly the federation-doctrine violation the corpus's no-unifier-without-laundering ([[project-no-unifier-without-laundering]]) discipline already names: a single vocabulary spread across two refusal kernels does not preserve either kernel's refusal surface.

### Secondary: ALIAS-RISK on "sacrifice"

The word *sacrifice* carries (at least) three distinct registers across the audit territory, two of them in-corpus and one relay-introduced. NI itself does not use the word at all.

| Sense | Where | What it names |
|---|---|---|
| **(a) Capability burned by the system** | AAG outcome-axis ("Sacrificial: survives by burning structure / capability / standing"); AAG mechanism-axis ("Sacrificial escape: exit by losing capability"); cluster clause #4 (sacrificial-survival laundering) | *Self-imposed* structural cost paid by the system to exit an inadmissible basin. The cost is metabolized internally; the audit refuses calling it restoration. |
| **(b) Burden imposed on the refinement-requester** | NI's mechanism (using *cost* / *discipline* / *social cost* / *contractual penalty* / *psychiatric* vocabulary — **NI does not use "sacrifice"**) | *Externally-imposed* cost paid by the actor who tries to refine the coarse map. The cost is metabolized by the refinement-requester (career damage, financial penalty, involuntary commitment); the venue stays at the coarse map. |
| **(c) Floor of witness-capability below which no admissible witness can be produced** | Relay-introduced ("sacrifice-floor"); not present in NI or AAG | Conjectured structural-cost floor at which the witness-production required by an effect threshold becomes structurally unaffordable. Refused by Pilot #9 as a gap-filler for the composition-axis. |

Sense (a) and sense (b) describe opposite directionality of cost (system-burns-self vs. external-actor-burns-refinement-requester). Sense (c) is the relay's lexical bridge between (a) and (b) that does not survive examination at either altitude.

NI is structurally about sense (b); AAG is structurally about sense (a); the relay's "sacrifice-floor" was sense (c) using sense (a)'s vocabulary to import sense (b)'s burden-shape into a composition-axis gap. The ALIAS-RISK is the relay's vocabulary move, not a fracture inside the corpus's own primitives.
