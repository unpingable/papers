## 2. The keeper line, and why it is a to-do list

> **The theory of why nobody cares is the theory of what to do about it.**

The corpus does not say "beg for care." It says pay the bridge: **failure specimen ·
war-story phrasing · receipt demo · NQ observatory · ops-facing autopsy.**

This is the public-register §8 no-single-slogan result restated as work: there is no
universal door because a one-slogan pitch would be a rhetorical global scalar, and the
same theorem that refuses the global scalar *prices each audience's bridge*. Illegibility
is not an audience defect to lament; it is a set of unpaid crossings, each with a known
toll. The four lanes above are the toll booths.

## 3. Standing correction (operator-witness)

Twenty-seven years of ops is standing. Not institutional permission to mutate employer
systems — standing as an **operator-witness**: standing to testify that production is
haunted, and to write the autopsy in receipts language. The prior framing was
self-abasing (as if only institutional labs are licensed to notice); this one is not.
Ops remains provenance, not boundary — but provenance with witness standing, which is
exactly the kind of standing the corpus knows how to type.
