## The verdict: publishable-novel, but much narrower than the fun version

The surviving paper is **not** "Semantic Prions," not "LLMs sound fake," not the taxonomy,
not "we discovered rhetorical inflation." It is one thing:

> **An oracle-grounded measure of *epistemic overdraft*: perceived inferential weight
> minus recoverable licensed increment, tested with true-triage / false-triage frame
> conditions in constructed micro-worlds with a known dependency structure.**

The biohazard-sticker version got attention; the publishable version puts on safety
goggles and stops saying "plague velvet" in front of reviewers.

## Prior-art wall (codex-verified 2026-06-22 against primary sources)

The "contentless rigor-cue inflates judgment" lane is **occupied**. Cannot sell inflation.

- **Eriksson 2012**, *The nonsense math effect* (J. Decision Making, journal.sjdm.org/12/12102/) — closest: a meaningless equation boosts judged abstract quality. The generic inflation effect *is this*.
- **Pennycook et al. 2015**, pseudo-profound bullshit receptivity (journal.sjdm.org/15/15923a/) — perceived profundity of content-free stimuli; also owns reader-heterogeneity / cognitive-style moderation.
- **Langer, Blank & Chanowitz 1978** — "placebic information," the copy-machine "because"; vacuous reason still moves compliance. The canonical warrant-without-content result.
- **Weisberg et al. 2008** — seductive allure of neuroscience explanations; irrelevant rigor-flavor inflates explanation quality.
- **Bott & Noveck 2004**, scalar-implicature truth-judgment tasks (doi.org/10.1016/j.cognition.2003.10.006) — **the precise ancestor of the micro-world + forced-choice design**: constructed contexts where readers separate literal truth from licensed pragmatic inference. A gift, not a threat — gives the design a 20-year validated lineage instead of "invented experimental pragmatics in a Waffle House parking lot."
- **Bharadwaj et al. 2025** (arXiv:2506.05339) — preference models over-reward length/structure/jargon/vagueness/sycophancy over substance. The LLM hook is **motivation, not contribution**.

## Contribution (what to actually sell)

1. Constructed micro-worlds with a known dependency structure.
2. True-triage vs false-triage rhetorical frames (e.g. deploy iff A∧B, A satisfied, B unknown → "the real question is whether **B**" = true triage; "...whether **A**" = false triage, performs crux-finding while pointing at a non-crux).
3. Measure **rigor lift** separately from **licensed-content recovery**.
4. Define **epistemic overdraft** = the mismatch (a high-weight frame writing a larger epistemic check than the recoverable content can cash).
5. Free response → calibration phase only; **forced-choice is the confirmatory instrument** (correct licensed takeaway vs plausible-unlicensed inference vs pure posture restatement vs distractor).

## Do NOT claim

- "These phrases are bad."
- "LLMs invented this."
- "Readers are fooled by fancy prose." / "We discovered rhetorical inflation."
- Reader heterogeneity / disagreement-as-DV is novel (it's a modest operational twist, mostly standard — demote to analysis choice, not contribution).

## Do claim

- "We can distinguish valid pragmatic framing from counterfeit analytic posture **when the underlying dependency structure is known**."
- "The target is not emptiness; it is **weight mismatch**."
- "A high-weight frame is illicit when it writes a larger epistemic check than the recoverable content can cash."

## The fatal reviewer objection (and the load-bearing defense)

> "Your forced-choice 'licensed content' answer key just stipulates *your* pragmatics —
> you've reduced rhetoric to logic-comprehension and graded against your own key."

Defense — and it's *why the constructed micro-world is load-bearing, not cosmetic*:

> Not in the confirmatory arm. The micro-world supplies a **formal dependency oracle**. If
> deployment depends on A∧B, A is satisfied, and B is unknown, then B is the crux **by
> construction** — a truth of the world, not a pragmatic stipulation. (This is exactly how
> Bott-Noveck's implicature paradigm survives the same objection.)

Corollary discipline: **stay in constructed worlds for the confirmatory study.** Natural
claims revert the "correct recovery" to experimenter judgment → objection becomes fatal
again. Natural examples are an explicitly **exploratory / ecological-extension** arm, never
the thing carrying validity. And concede openly that for triage frames in formal worlds the
recovery task *is* a logic-comprehension task — the contribution is the **dissociation**
between fluency/inflation rating and that ground-truthed comprehension, measured as
overdraft. Framed as "we measure whether rhetoric is real," it gets killed.

## Structural footnote (optional, keep implicit for an empirical venue)

Overdraft is the corpus's **missing-conversion-witness / no-free-lift** rule pointed at
prose: a frame that raises authority without a recoverable witness is a laundering move;
"cash the check the frame writes" = the conversion-witness binding rule. This is a *new
empirical surface* (the instrument doesn't exist — a real forcing case), NOT a fake-mustache
return of a kernel, so the tombstone discipline doesn't bite. For a pragmatics/NLP-eval
venue keep this connection out of the paper; it's a bridge to draw later if the empirical
work is ever tied back to the formal corpus. See `anti-laundering-doctrine-map.md`.
