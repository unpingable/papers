# Memory Decay and Reliance Readout

**Filed:** 2026-06-15. **Status:** doctrine note / **candidate specimen, NOT a new kernel.**
Name-early, overlap-gated. The refusals here are MEMORY-FLAVORED instances of the
already-built readout family — do not mint a `MemoryReliance` kernel (see § Overlap). Physics
analogy not needed. This is the **witnessed copy**: written because the memory of how this
arrived is more fragile than the idea, and an unwitnessed recollection of it is not
admissible.

## The corrected spine

Not "confidence lies" — rhetorically good, doctrinally dirty (it smuggles a liar into the
machine; the disease was never deception).

> **Confidence does not lie. Confidence exceeds jurisdiction.**

The failure is **unauthorized conversion**, not falsehood. Confidence is a press office
issuing badges it has no authority to print.

## Held

- Memory is **latent, lossy, reconstructive** — not a stored record played back.
- **Remembering may decay; reliance is read out.** (Decay is diachronic substrate; reliance
  is the synchronic, authorized collapse — see [[standing-as-readout-no-free-standing-readout]].)
- **Freshness** asks whether the claim still describes the world.
- **Memory fidelity** asks whether the *record of observation* can be trusted. Different axis
  from freshness (which is evidence-side metric time; see attack-04 / decay-family).
- Confidence is admissible **only as introspective report** ("this feels available / vivid /
  certain"), never as certification ("this was witnessed / sourced / safe to rely on").
- **Confidence is not custody. Fluency is not provenance. Recall is not reliance.**
- The reliance gate keys on **fidelity, custody/provenance, corroboration, and disqualifiers**
  — not on confidence.
- **Assistant/user recollection is not witnessed absent a durable source.**

## The refusal ladder

```text
Confidence     ⊬ Fidelity
Fluency        ⊬ Provenance
Recall         ⊬ Reliance
HighConfidence ⊬ MayRely
```

Admitted role, narrow:

```text
Confidence MAY report:    "this feels available / vivid / certain"
Confidence MAY NOT certify:"this was witnessed / sourced / safe to rely on"
```

## Live specimens

- **Human memory** — vivid, confident, reconstructive; flashbulb memories are high-confidence
  low-fidelity. The textbook case of `HighConfidence ⊬ MayRely`.
- **Assistant memory** — fluent recall of "what we decided" without a durable source is
  fluency, not provenance. This session's own auto-memory (always-loaded pointers) is the
  custody layer; the working notes are the witnessed records it points to.
