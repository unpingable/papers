## Center of gravity

Premature/belated duality and the empty binding window. Two curves on the temporal axis — m(t) admissibility maturity, c(t) consequence viability — and the valid binding window W = { t | m(t) ≥ θ_m ∧ c(t) ≥ θ_c }. Premature: binding at t with m(t) < θ_m. Belated: binding at t with c(t) < θ_c. Empty-window: W = ∅. The discriminator that distinguishes empty-window failure from ordinary process delay is *residual event-class mismatch* under a declared counterfactual regime.

## Position in the series

P26 sits as the *temporal* sibling to P25's *spatial* result. P25 names substitution forced under observability asymmetry; P26 names temporal-seam failure under maturity/consequence detachment. Both negative results in the P22→P26 spine.
